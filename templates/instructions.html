Instructions


How to use this tool?

Just follow these few steps:

  1. Make sure your data have the right format (instructions will be given)
  2. Upload your input data
  3. Click on "process data"
  4. You are done! You will be landing on the final page.
What are the output?

The output are:

  1. Multiple dynamic charts
  2. Downloadable output file

The output charts present the total spring discharge as provided by the user and the calculated baseflow, along with statistics!

If you would like to see other outputs, do not hesitate to contact us!
