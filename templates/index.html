About


What is this website for?

The purpose of this website is to give you - water industry professionals, researchers, students - a quick and efficient tool to determine the baseflow of a karst system.

Along with the calculation of the raw output data, dynamic charts are provided to enable to visualise them.
Check out the demo page to see what you can get with your own data!

Why calculate baseflow?

Baseflow is a streamflow component which reacts slowly to rainfall and is usually associated with water discharged from groundwater storage.

Knowledge about baseflow is useful in the assessment of water quality and low-flow conditions. It can also be used to calibrate or validate hydrological models.
